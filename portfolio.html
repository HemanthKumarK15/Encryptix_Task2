Project 1

PROJECT TITLE:: EYES OF JUSTICE: FACE DETECTION AND RECOGNITION FOR CRIME PREVENTION

Role: Team Leader

Project Overview


Eyes of Justice is an advanced face detection and recognition system designed to enhance crime prevention efforts. Utilizing cutting-edge artificial intelligence and machine learning algorithms, this project aims to assist law enforcement agencies in identifying and tracking suspects, thereby improving public safety and security. The process of identifying and spotting a criminal is slow and difficult. Criminals, these days are getting smarter by not leaving any form of biological evidence or fingerprint impressions on the crime scene. A quick and easy solution is using state-of-the-art face identification systems. With the advancement in security technology, CCTV cameras are being installed at most of the buildings and traffic lights for surveillance purposes. The video footage from the camera can be used to identify suspects, criminals, runaways, missing persons etc. This paper explores a way to develop a criminal identification system using ML and deep neural networks. The following method can be used as an elegant way to make law enforcement hassle-free.

Key Features

Real-Time Face Detection: Employs sophisticated AI models to accurately detect faces in real-time from surveillance footage and public cameras.

Facial Recognition: Matches detected faces against a comprehensive database of known offenders, missing persons, and persons of interest, providing instant identification.

PROJECT TITLE:: BEACH CLEANUP TOOL

Role: Team Leader

Project Overview


Beach cleanup tool Design a beach cleanup tool(s) which anyone can use on-demand. Parts of the problem to be addressed:
● it should be easy to use by an individual
● it should be cost effective
● it shouldn’t collect sand
● it shouldn’t disturb the ecosystem
● it should be easy to dump the collected garbage (things to consider: hygiene)
● materials used should be durable (things to consider: coastal weather)
This could be a local or municipality initiative in a few beaches to encourage the local public to clean while they walk and dump on their way out. It can be done using the same tech as Yulu using an app with pick up and drop points at entry and exit places of beach.

Project 3

PROJECT TITLE:: REPOSITORY OF INTERNSHIP DETAILS

Role: Team Leader

Project Overview


The Repository of Internship Details is a comprehensive platform designed to manage and store information about internships. This project aims to facilitate students, educational institutions, and companies by providing a centralized database where internship opportunities, experiences, and feedback can be documented, accessed, and managed efficiently. Key Features Internship Listings: A searchable database of available internships from various companies and organizations, including detailed descriptions, requirements, and application deadlines. Student Profiles: Allows students to create and manage profiles showcasing their skills, academic background, and previous internship experiences. Company Profiles: Companies can create profiles to list their internship opportunities and provide information about their organization and the benefits they offer to interns. Project Benefits For Students: Provides easy access to a wide range of internship opportunities, helps in building a professional network, and offers insights through peer reviews. For Companies: Streamlines the process of finding and managing potential interns, and enhances visibility among students from various educational institutions. For Educational Institutions: Simplifies the tracking and management of student internships, ensuring students gain relevant industry experience aligned with their academic programs.
